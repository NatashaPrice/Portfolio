Cat & Octo, Weather Pets

Cat & Octo is a game and a weather app in one. Currently, a first prototype is being built, with simple functionality to pull weather data and represent it in a fun way. With iterations down the road, a more complex pet game will incorporate real-time weather with taking care of an outdoor pet. One pet is a cat and one pet is an octopus. With patience and care, you will accumulate points to make your pets happier. Giving them the proper clothes, shelter, sunscreen, and treats will ensure a happy pet.

1. The first screen you can find the Cat & Octo icon, please click the icon and go through to the welcome screen.
2. If you click/press on the welcome screen you will be taken to the launch screen. 3. Press the launch screen to go to cat screen, this screen shows screens for day, night, and rain.
4. Click to go to Octopus screen.
5. Click to go to the hourly weather for the day.
6. Click to go to the week's weather forecast.

This is a simplified prototype, version 1.0. My friend, who is a programmer, is working on the back end code in "Xcode", a program for prototyping an iPhone app. The last meeting we had he said he has chosen an API, it stands for Application Programming Interface, and the one he chose is specifically for sending weather data to the application. It will let us know the current weather based on location. For our first working prototype we will stick with just Berkeley, but expand to update based on wherever you are. I would like to include moon phases in a later version.
Once we have spent a lot of time on our first app, possible other pets could be made. One suggestion for future pets is "Frog & Dog"
